2017 was our seventeenth year for an animated Christmas Lights Display. This year we focused on increasing the number of songs and made just a few technical adjustments vs. the previous year. The most visible change was replacing the incandescent [countdown clock](/technology/clock) with an LED-based design.

We also invested in a much sturdier frame for the [18x8 foot grid](/technology/grid) that hangs above the door while nearly doubling the number of songs.

This year was amazing with over 30 neighbors joining to celebrate as the clock hit zero. Hopefully you'll join us next year!
