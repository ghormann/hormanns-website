2020 was our 20th year for an animated Christmas Lights Display. This year we added five new songs, the ability to vote for the type of [snowmen](/technology/snowmen/), and the [new sign above the garage door](/technology/info-board/) that displays the radio station and power being consumed by the display.

For the last fifteen years or so, a number of neighbors have braved the cold to watch the clock hit midnight and welcome Christmas. While we didn't record the event, we greatly appreciate all of those who came out this year for a one of a kind celebration.
