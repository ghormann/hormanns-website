2024 was our 24th year for an animated Christmas Lights Display. With over 65,125 lights synchronized
to 68 minutes of Christmas music (26 songs)Th. is year we updated
the effects on about half of the songs, added push button controls to the tunnel, and added the song
[Sounding Joy](https://youtu.be/-dVyRL2xsIk) to the list.

We welcomed in Christmas morning with 50 neighbors by counting down the last seconds to Christmas in
front of the [countdown clock](/technology/clock/). The [video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ceJZGeCeO78)
of the evening shows the excitement. Make your plans now to join us next year!
