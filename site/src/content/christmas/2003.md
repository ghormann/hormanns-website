2003 was our third year for an animated Christmas display. The [320 Circuit Controller](/technology/parallel-320/) was used again this year. Due to the birth of our first child, we didn't make many changes from last year. The only viewer-noticeable differences are (a) the addition of more lights on the bushes and (b) changing the sign below the countdown clock to be more descriptive. These changes pushed us from ~6,000 lights to just over 13,000.
