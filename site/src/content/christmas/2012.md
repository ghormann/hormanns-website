2012 was our twelfth year for an animated Christmas display. We put a few additional 8-port [LOR Hobbyist Controllers](http://www.lightorama.com/) into service. Two of the controllers replaced the 320-controllers on the sign above the door, while a third is used to control the new blue flood lights that were added this year. The changes are not that significant from the last few years, but the videos have all been updated in 2012 allowing users to see the differences.

For the last 7 years or so, a small (but growing) group of neighbors have braved the cold to watch the clock hit mid-night and welcome Christmas. This year, we had about 20 individuals standing across the street watching. I even got Mary to come outside this year! This has been a great tradition.
