2010 was our tenth year for an animated Christmas display. Unfortunately, very little changed from the previous year. We did order a few more [LOR Controllers](http://www.lightorama.com/) so with any amount of luck I'll finally build a new addition next year.

For the last 6 years or so, a small (but growing) group of neighbors have braved the cold to watch the clock hit midnight and welcome Christmas. This year, we had about 30 individuals standing across the street watching. The noise was loud enough to wake up my wife. This has been a great tradition. Hopefully one of these years, I'll get her to actually come out!
