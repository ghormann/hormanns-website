2018 is our eighteenth year for an animated Christmas Lights Display. This year we added the 43 mini-trees in the front lawn. As each one of the 90 pixels on each tree is individually controllable, we've added a number of more enjoyable effects on the yard. In addition, we added a feature where viewers could [send their first name via text message](/technology/text-message/) and have it display below the clock and on the big grid.

For the last thirteen years or so, a number of neighbors have braved the cold to watch the clock hit midnight and welcome Christmas. ([Video](https://youtu.be/mzx8egIFU8U))

This year was amazing with over 48 neighbors joining to celebrate as the clock hit zero. Hopefully you'll join us next year!
