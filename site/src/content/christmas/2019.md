2019 was our ninetieth year for an animated Christmas Lights Display. This year we added high-def grids to the windows, a second mega tree, and completely rebuilt the [the showmen](/technology/snowmen/). In addition, we added a feature where viewers could [vote for the next song](/technology/voting/) to be played.

For the last fourteen years or so, a number of neighbors have braved the cold to watch the clock hit midnight and welcome Christmas. This year we had ([45 neighbors join](https://youtu.be/KzlcXVM5kTE)) to celebrate as the clock hit zero.
