2025 is our 25th year for an animated Christmas Lights Display. With over 75,805 lights synchronized to 73 minutes of Christmas music (28 songs), it is well worth the trip to
Liberty Township to see it in person (we are located on [Devon Drive](#house_address)). We are still adding the videos for those out of town, but
the videos don't capture the nearly as well as being in person. This year we replaced the lights on the bushes with a 48ft x 4 ft grid just in front of the house and updated every song to take advantage of this new prop. We also added spinners on each side of the garage door.
