2011 was our eleventh year for an animated Christmas display. I did order a few more [LOR Controllers](http://www.lightorama.com/) but never got to put them to use...

For the last 7 years or so, a small (but growing) group of neighbors have braved the cold to watch the clock hit mid-night and welcome Christmas. This year, we had about 20 individuals standing across the street watching. This has been a great tradition.
