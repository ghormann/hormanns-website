2022 was our 22th year for an animated Christmas Lights Display. Construction started November 10th and we went live November 23rd.
This year we added the tunnel on the right and move the countdown clock to the left of the display. We had about 20 individuals join
us to see the countdown clock hit zero. (It was bitter cold!)
