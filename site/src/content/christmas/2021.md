2021 was our 21th year for an animated Christmas Lights Display. This year we added two new songs (
[You make it feel like Christmas](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yYflTRQp4Bg) and [Christmas Can Can](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dYW9W9nW_WM))
along with Tom Nook as a possible [snowmen](/technology/snowmen/).
We also added two additional [information panels](/technology/info-board/) on either side of the display to communicate the donation drop off point
as well as the radio station.
We
planned to add some more elements above the garage, but the pixels we ordered were significantly delayed and didn't arrive in time.

For the last sixteen years or so, a number of neighbors have braved the cold to watch the clock hit midnight and welcome Christmas. This year was amazing with over 45 neighbors joining to celebrate as the clock hit zero. Hopefully you'll join us next year!
